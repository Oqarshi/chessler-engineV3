Chessler V1

Features:

  • Can play as white or black
  • User is able to resign
  • Detectable 50 move rule, stalemate, checkmate, 3 fold repetition
  • Play custom position via FEN
  • 10 Different time controls for Engine
  • Ability to take back a move
  • Able to change board color to 3 different colors
  • Goes to depth 8 in 1 second
  • Calculates about 860,000 nodes in 1 second

Known Bugs:

  • Cannot flip board
  • Score or Eval is not accurate
  • Cannot drag and drop pieces
  • Pieces do not light up on click when board color is changed
  • Board misaligned by 1-3 pixels
  • Only 10 different time controls for Engine
  • Does not record moves
  • No Analysis Feature

Chessler V2

Features:

  • Can play as white or black
  • Custom time control for the engine *
  • Detectable 50-move rule, stalemate, checkmate, 3-fold repetition
  • Ability to play custom positions via FEN
  • Ability to flip the board *
  • Ability to take back a move
  • Able to drag and drop pieces *
  • Goes to depth 9 in 1 second *
  • Calculates about 1,443,066 nodes in 1 second *
  • Pieces light up on click *
  • Analysis feature added *
  • Records moves while the game is being played
  • Beats Stockfish lvl 6 and draws Stockfish lvl 7. Beats 3200 rated Komodo

Known Bugs:

  • Drag and dropping is a bit buggy
  • Piece images are lower quality -
  • The engine breaks if the user tries to move the engine's piece while the engine is thinking -
  • Move notation can break if the user takes back a move
  • Cannot change board color -
